Project Info

We are an association focused on fixing potholes around Bétera to make the streets safer and more comfortable for everyone.Now, we are raising money because the city council has given us the permits, but has told us to collect half of the budget to be able to start our project.

Projects

Before Bétera, we were in more remote towns in Castilla-La Mancha and Castilla y León to gain some practice and because it was easy to reach agreements with the local councils.We also want to speak with the town councils of Naquera and Serra because we have received many complaints about the state of the roads in these towns, and we are seeking the happiness of the villagers.
